How I Got Into Coding

It all started with curiosity. Watching YouTube tutorials and building small projects made me fall in love with the logic and creativity behind coding. Every bug is a puzzle, and every fix is a win.

My Typical Day in College

A day at college is a mix of classes, chai breaks, coding practice, and sometimes badminton. Group studies and last-minute submissions add to the chaos but also make it memorable.

Books That Changed My Thinking

Books like *Atomic Habits* and *Deep Work* completely shifted my mindset. They taught me the value of focus, routine, and how small changes lead to big results over time.

Why I Started Working Out

Staring at screens all day made me realize the importance of physical health. Just 30 minutes of home workouts or playing badminton gives me more energy and mental clarity.

Building a Growth Mindset

Failures used to demotivate me, but now I see them as feedback. Whether it's a bug that took hours or a project that didn’t work out, everything teaches something valuable.
